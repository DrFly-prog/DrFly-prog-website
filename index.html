Tangram Infinity

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Overview

Tangram Infinity ("the App") is a puzzle game developed by an independent creator. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

Data We Collect

The App itself does not collect any personal information. However, we use third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob — used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve relevant ads.
  • Game progress — your score and game state are stored locally on your device only using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Advertising

The App displays ads through Google AdMob, including interstitial ads and rewarded video ads. AdMob may use cookies and similar technologies to collect data for ad personalization.

We request non-personalized ads where possible. For more information on how Google uses data from apps that use AdMob, please visit: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by our advertising partner (Google AdMob) as described above.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Data Retention

Local game data (score, saved game) is stored on your device and can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the App. We do not retain any data on our own servers.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data collected about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising (via your device settings)

On Android, you can opt out of personalized ads in Settings → Google → Ads. On iOS, go to Settings → Privacy → Tracking.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
